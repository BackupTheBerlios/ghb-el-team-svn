Please add your name in square brackets next to the files below you would like
to take on translating.

[UN] stands for Update Needed and files marked as such
should be left for later review, let us first translate the rest of the
handbook and we deal with those in due course. FYI - the list of existing
translations has been taken from Gentoo Bug (#99170) at:
http://bugs.gentoo.org/show_bug.cgi?id=99170

Any questions please raise them with the mailing list
at ghb-el-team@lists.berlios.de.

Thank you for your time and effort and good luck translating!
